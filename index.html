Senior Sistemas it's one of the majors software developer companies in Brazil, known mainly because of it's Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) software named Sapiens.

We (applied research team) are working on a large scale recommender engine applied to B2B model. But before I starting writing a little deeper about some of my current dutties here, let me write a story to you.

When I was twelve-years old my brother was doing his bachelor's thesis which was programming a PacMac game using AgentSpeak language. He was so annoyed with me bothering them all the time about it, that someday he said to me: - "Ok, Felipe! you won. I'll teach how to programming, but you have to promiss me that you will stay focused and do as I as I. We gotta deal?" And a I said:
- "Ok, we gotta a deal, man! thanks for believe me!". And that was the very beginning of my journey as a software developer.

That experience with my brother it touched me, and inspired me to conquer my bachelor degree in Information Systems at Universidade do Estado de Santa Catarina (UDESC).
