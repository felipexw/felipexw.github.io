We (applied research team) are working on a large scale recommender engine applied to B2B model. Well, before I start writing a little deeper about some of my dutties here, let me write a little to you.

He was so annoyed with me bothering them all the time about it, that someday he said to me: - "Ok, Felipe! you won.
